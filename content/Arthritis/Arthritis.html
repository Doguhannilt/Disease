Arthritis Guide

What is Arthritis?

Arthritis is a group of disorders characterized by joint inflammation. It causes pain, swelling, and stiffness in the joints, which can affect daily activities and reduce the quality of life.

Types of Arthritis

There are many types of arthritis, but some common ones include:

  • Osteoarthritis
  • Rheumatoid Arthritis
  • Juvenile Arthritis
  • Gout
  • Ankylosing Spondylitis

Symptoms of Arthritis

Symptoms may vary depending on the type of arthritis, but common signs include joint pain, stiffness, swelling, and reduced range of motion. In some cases, arthritis can affect other organs as well.

Treatment and Management

While there is no cure for arthritis, various treatments aim to reduce pain, improve joint function, and slow disease progression. Treatment options may include medications, physical therapy, lifestyle changes, and in some cases, surgery.
